# 🚀 Professional Trader-Grade Features Roadmap

## 🎯 HIGH PRIORITY - Core Trading Features

### 1. **Portfolio Management & Tracking** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why:** Essential for any professional trader
- **Portfolio Dashboard**
  - Real-time portfolio value
  - P&L tracking (realized/unrealized)
  - Position sizing visualization
  - Allocation by sector/industry
  - Performance vs benchmarks (S&P 500, NASDAQ)
  
- **Position Management**
  - Add/edit/remove positions
  - Entry/exit price tracking
  - Cost basis calculation
  - Dividend tracking
  - Split adjustments
  
- **Portfolio Analytics**
  - Portfolio-level risk metrics
  - Correlation analysis
  - Diversification score
  - Sector concentration risk
  - Beta-weighted portfolio exposure

### 2. **Advanced Position Sizing Calculator** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why:** Critical for risk management
- **Kelly Criterion Calculator**
  - Optimal position sizing based on win rate
  - Risk-adjusted position sizing
  
- **Volatility-Based Sizing**
  - ATR-based position sizing
  - Volatility-adjusted position sizes
  
- **Portfolio-Level Sizing**
  - Maximum portfolio heat
  - Correlation-adjusted sizing
  - Sector limits

### 3. **Real-Time Alerts & Notifications** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why:** Professional traders need instant notifications
- **Price Alerts**
  - Price target alerts (above/below)
  - Percentage change alerts
  - Breakout/breakdown alerts
  
- **Technical Alerts**
  - RSI overbought/oversold
  - MACD crossovers
  - Moving average crossovers
  - Volume spikes
  
- **Fundamental Alerts**
  - Score changes
  - Earnings announcements
  - Analyst rating changes
  - News sentiment shifts
  
- **Delivery Methods**
  - In-app notifications
  - Email alerts
  - SMS (via Twilio)
  - Webhook integration

### 4. **Watchlist Management** ⭐⭐
**Why:** Essential for tracking multiple stocks
- **Multiple Watchlists**
  - Create custom watchlists
  - Sector-based watchlists
  - Strategy-based watchlists
  
- **Watchlist Features**
  - Real-time price updates
  - Quick access to analysis
  - Custom columns/metrics
  - Sorting and filtering
  - Export watchlists

### 5. **Trade Journal & Performance Analytics** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why:** Critical for improving trading performance
- **Trade Journal**
  - Log all trades (entry/exit)
  - Trade notes and rationale
  - Screenshots/charts
  - Tag trades by strategy
  
- **Performance Analytics**
  - Win rate by strategy
  - Average win/loss
  - Risk-reward ratios
  - Monthly/yearly performance
  - Drawdown analysis
  - Strategy comparison

### 6. **Enhanced Technical Analysis** ⭐⭐
**Why:** Professional traders need advanced indicators
- **Additional Indicators**
  - Stochastic Oscillator
  - Williams %R
  - ADX (Average Directional Index)
  - OBV (On-Balance Volume)
  - Ichimoku Cloud
  - Fibonacci Retracements

  - Chart patterns (head & shoulders, triangles)
  - Support/resistance auto-detection
  
- **Volume Analysis**
  - Volume profile
  - VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)
  - Volume trend analysis
  - Unusual volume detection

**Why:** Many professional traders use options

- **Options Strategies**

  - Protective puts
  - Spreads (bull/bear)
  - Straddles/Strangles
  - Strategy P&L calculator

### 8. **Backtesting Engine** ⭐⭐
**Why:** Test strategies before risking capital
- **Strategy Backtesting**
  - Define custom strategies
  - Historical performance simulation
  - Walk-forward optimization
  - Monte Carlo simulation
  
- **Performance Metrics**
  - Sharpe ratio
  - Sortino ratio
  - Maximum drawdown
  - Win rate
  - Profit factor

### 9. **Correlation & Sector Analysis** ⭐⭐
**Why:** Understand market relationships
- **Correlation Matrix**
  - Stock-to-stock correlations

  - Market correlation (Beta)
  - Rolling correlation analysis
  
- **Sector Rotation Analysis**
  - Sector performance tracking
  - Sector momentum
  - Relative strength by sector
  - Sector allocation recommendations

### 10. **Market Sentiment Indicators** ⭐
**Why:** Gauge market mood
- **Sentiment Metrics**
  - Put/Call ratio
